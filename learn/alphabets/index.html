Alphabets

A - Make a fist with your thumb extended and place it near your forehead.

B - Make a closed fist with your index finger extended and tap it against the palm of your other hand.

C - Make a C-shape with your hand and move it in a circular motion.

D - Make a fist with your index finger and thumb extended, and tap your temple twice.

E - Extend all fingers on one hand and tap your chest.

F - Make a fist with your thumb extended and move it across your forehead.

G - Make a fist with your pinky extended and move it in a circular motion.

H - Hold your hand in a flat, open position with all fingers together.

I - Point your index finger straight up.

J - Make a hook shape with your index finger.

K - Make a fist with your index and middle fingers extended and crossed.

L - Extend your thumb and index finger into an L shape.

M - Make a fist with your thumb extended and place it near your chin.

N - Make a fist with your index and middle fingers extended and tap them against your chest.

O - Form a circle with your thumb and index finger.

P - Make a fist with your pinky extended and move it in a circular motion.

Q - Make a fist with your index finger extended and draw a Q shape in the air.

R - Make a hook shape with your index and middle fingers.

S - Extend your fingers and hold your hand in a flat, open position.

T - Make a fist with your thumb extended and tap it against your chest.

U - Make a U shape with your index and middle fingers.

V - Make a V shape with your index and middle fingers.

W - Make a W shape with your thumb, index, and middle fingers.

X - Cross your index and middle fingers.

Y - Extend your pinky and thumb, and hold your other fingers down.

Z - Make a Z shape with your index finger and thumb.
